Frequently Asked Questions

Brand

Man Series is a clinical, science-backed skincare brand designed and formulated specifically for Indian men. We focus on transparent active percentages, skin minimalism (fewer products, healthier skin), and honest pricing. You can read more about our philosophy and story in the About section.

Shipping details

We ship all across India, delivering to over 20,000 pincodes. Orders can be paid via prepaid digital methods or Cash on Delivery (COD) for your convenience. All domestic orders are dispatched from our centralized fulfillment center in Jaipur, Rajasthan.

Delivery details

We usually ship within 24 working hours after receiving your order. Once shipped, our logistics partner typically takes between 2 to 5 business days to deliver the package. Orders in metropolitan areas and Tier-1 cities can expect a faster delivery speed (often within 3 days).

Returns details

You can raise a return request by sending us an email at support@manseries.in, and our support team will assist you. Return shipments are managed entirely by us. For full details on criteria and eligibility, please check our dedicated Returns & Refund Policy page.
